Chapter G — CBORD → DirectX Device Vectorization

TopicDescription
CBORD DX Device Creation CBORD creates the Direct3D device: 

D3D11CreateDevice(
    nullptr,
    D3D_DRIVER_TYPE_HARDWARE,
    nullptr,
    flags,
    nullptr,0,
    D3D11_SDK_VERSION,
    &dx->d3d,
    nullptr,
    &dx->ctx
);
Swap Chain Vector CBORD builds the swap chain: 

factory->CreateSwapChain(dx->d3d,&desc,&dx->swap);
Render Target Vector 

dx->d3d->CreateRenderTargetView(backBuffer,nullptr,&dx->rtv);
Vector Export CBORD exports: 

avis.spine.dx = dx;
This is the **primary DirectX vector** for the entire engine.
